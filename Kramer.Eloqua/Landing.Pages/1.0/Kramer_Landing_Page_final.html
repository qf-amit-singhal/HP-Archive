How Medicare and a Medicare Supplement plan work together to give you the best healthcare coverage.

What is Medicare?

  • Medicare is a health
    insurance program
    provided by the
    government for the people
    age 65 or older.

  • Medicare helps with
    the cost of health care,
    but it doesn't cover all medical expenses.

  • It also doesn't cover
    most long-term care.

What is a Medicare Supplement Plan?

  • A Supplement plan works
    alongside your Orginal
    Medicare.

  • It covers out-of-pocket
    costs not covered, such
    as copayments,
    coininsurance, and deductables.

  • It fills in "gaps" for basic benefits not covered.

Medicare Supplement plans can help you manage your health and costs:

  • Reduce out-of pocket costs.

  • Choose your medicare doctor and your hospital

  • Nationwide coverage

  • Get the help paying for deductables, copayments, and coinsurance
