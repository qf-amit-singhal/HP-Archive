"Qualfon is in it for the long term. It’s not just about making each person’s life better, but to make more people’s lives better over the next 5, 10, 50 years. As leaders we are paid for three things and three things only: our judgement, our integrity, and our character. The way to treat everyone, not just at work or home, but everyone, treat everyone with fairness dignity and respect.

The scope of our impact on the world isn’t just the 15,000 people that work for Qualfon and their families, it goes well beyond that over the years. That makes it an exciting place to be in. If you learn and do the right things and take advantage of opportunities, the sky is the limit. As we grow and continue to look for more opportunity for good supervisors and team leads and operations managers and next thing you know somebody does a good job as the director of operations.

One thing that can never be taken away from you is your education. We help people get an education and make their lives better not only for themselves, but also for their family. With that, they’re able to move forward with whatever their calling is in life. Giving them that start is very exciting."

On 7/9/2021, I will have been employed with Qualfon for 9 years. “I’ve been with this company for about 14 years. Over the years I have seen tons of change happen to our organization. The most amazing change happened when we joined the Qualfon family. Qualfon is so unique due to the manner in which the organization is structured and how they invest in their employees. For example, I’ve never heard of a company provide so many opportunities for career growth, and helping the individual team members in EVERY aspect of their life. From meeting home needs to impacting our communities in a positive way. The company truly lives and breathes our mission to “Be the best and make each person’s life better”. I’m grateful for my career here at Qualfon”.
"In 2007 began my employment with Dialog Direct as an auditor for the Dependent Audit vertical of Customer Support Services, being promoted to Quality Control Analyst after 6 months. Then I transitioned to Program Manager for our inbound call center, which acted as a liaison between clients and the call center operations.

In 2011, Dialog Direct merged with NOVO 1, Inc. followed by DMI, GC Holdings and Allegra Direct. Our VP of Call Center Solutions and Human Resources VP at that time, approached me to partake in a large project to source, recruit and seat 250 call center agents for a large contract that was being seated in the Highland Park location. Shortly after the initial 250 agents were sat, the realization that there was going to be a larger, ongoing need for recruitment efforts transitioning out of utilizing staffing agencies, the Talent Acquisition Team was launched. In 2014 I moved from TA to a Human Resources/Benefits/Payroll hybrid role and subsequently to an HRIS/Benefits/Payroll roll in 2016. In 2020 I was assigned to the Payroll Administration role exclusively, ultimately being promoted to Sr. Payroll Administrator in 2021.

Throughout my tenure at Qualfon/Dialog Direct, I have had the pleasure to work with a plethora of teams and personalities. The most outstanding evolution that has occurred in the culture of the company is cohesiveness. Prior to the merge with NOVO 1, DMI and Allegra Direct, each department operated in a silo. There was no functional communication or collaboration between departments. Each department had their respective tasks and never the two shall meet for enhancement of processes and procedures. Fast forward to present day, Qualfon teams are consistently reviewing ownership in specific tasks and working as a unit to guarantee success for all departments. Personal relationships have flourished. The majority of individual team members hold a high standard in the output of their work and do not hesitate to offer assistance to one another, regardless of department, title or location. This is a company and pseudo family that I am proud to be a part of and cannot wait to see what the future of Qualfon will bring. "
